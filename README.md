# Bangla Calendar - বাংলা ক্যালেন্ডার

A free online Bangla calendar tool to view **today's Bangla date**, monthly calendars, holidays (ছুটির তালিকা), Bengali months, and Gregorian-Bangla date correspondence. Supports yearly navigation for past, present, and future Bangla years.

**Live Demo:** [https://calendar.netstudy.in/](https://calendar.netstudy.in/)

## Features

- Full monthly **Bangla Calendar** display with Bengali month names and dates
- Shows corresponding **English (Gregorian) dates** alongside Bangla dates
- Navigation for previous/next years (e.g., 2025, 2026, 2027 calendars)
- **Holiday List (ছুটির তালিকা)** – Important Bengali & national holidays marked
- Bengali year (বাংলা সন), months (বৈশাখ, জ্যৈষ্ঠ, আষাঢ় etc.), and structure explained
- Clean, simple interface – easy to read on mobile and desktop
- No signup or installation needed – 100% free and browser-based
- Useful for Bengalis in India (West Bengal, Tripura, Assam) and Bangladesh

## How to Use

1. Visit **[https://calendar.netstudy.in/](https://calendar.netstudy.in/)**
2. View the current or selected year's Bangla calendar grid
3. See Bengali month names, dates, and matching English dates
4. Check the **ছুটির তালিকা** section for holidays and important days
5. Use navigation links to switch between years (e.g., 2026 Bangla Calendar)
6. Perfect for planning festivals like Pohela Boishakh, Durga Puja, Eid, etc.

## Why Use This Tool?

- Quickly check **বাংলা কত তারিখ** or which Bengali month it is today
- Plan cultural events, pujas, or regional holidays accurately
- Understand the Bangla calendar structure (12 months starting from Boishakh)
- Free alternative to printed calendars or complex apps
- Helps with official purposes in Bengali-speaking regions
